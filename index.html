For the past 8 years, I've been deep in the world of cosmetic surgery recovery, sharing real experiences, education, and post-operative insights across YouTube, where my content has reached over 100,000 views. Over the past 5 years, I've also worked directly with post-operative patients through massage therapy and aesthetics, seeing firsthand how much recovery environment and technique matter.

After training at a CIDESCO-accredited institute and receiving advanced education in esthetics, massage therapy, post op massage techniques, hydrotherapy, and recovery modalities, I built a practice centered around post-operative care and corrective bodywork.

Advanced Fibrosis & Corrective Recovery

One of the most overlooked aspects of post-operative recovery is fibrosis. Many patients receive post op massage without a provider who truly understands how to identify and correct it. Through advanced Brazilian and Colombian recovery techniques and corrective bodywork, I focus on addressing the challenges that often go unresolved elsewhere.

I created The Sculpt Houston and The SnatchedPrint Method™ to provide the kind of recovery experience I believe every patient deserves.
